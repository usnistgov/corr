Integration

CoRR would like to place itself in front of the existing Simulation Management (SM) tools. By providing a robust and flexible API, CoRR opens a space of cloud federation for SM tools where scientits would have cloud spaces to store the records produces with these SM tools.

Collaboration

The core survival of a scientific investigation is in its management. By providing various ways to record scientific investigations (Web, Tools), CoRR is designed on top of a data model scheme tailored in relationships that is focused on maximizing the ease of managment for scientists. The CoRR frontend is implemented to deliver as much user experience as possible with a material design.

Management

Scientists registered in CoRR will be able to collaborate on records. Many schemes of collaborations are available. Starting from making a private record public to actually producing collaboration meta-data from interactions between scientists in CoRR.
